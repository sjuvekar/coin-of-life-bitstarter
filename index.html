What is Coin of Life? It's a simple board game involving coins.. that's all!

Coin of Life is a game based on Conway's Game of Life cellular automata. You start by placing some coins on an empty board. The coins grow by one golden rule:

A cell with two or three neighboring coins generates a new coin, otherwise it destroys its own coin!

Wow, that sounds complicated but it's not! Tap the tutorial link on the main screen to get a hang of it! Plus, here are some patterns you can place on the board to earn you more coins!
