What is Coin of Life?

Coin of Life is a game based on Conway's Game of Life cellular automata. You start by placing some coins on an empty board. The coins grow by two rules:

A cell with two or three neighboring coins generates its own coin

Otherwise, it destroys its own coin

Here are some patterns you can place on the board to earn you more coins!

How can I earn BitCoins by playing Coin of Life?

Every million coins earned on Coin of Life gives you a bitcoin! Although it appears a lot, you'll be surprised how quickly you accumulate coins!

There are some additional goodies! Earn gems, pearls and diamonds to multiply your coins! Don't forget to avoid collecting rocks unless you want to see your coins disappear!
